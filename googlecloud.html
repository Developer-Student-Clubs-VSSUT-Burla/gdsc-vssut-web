    • About the Program

    Developer Students Club, VSSUT in collaboration with Google Developers and Qwiklabs initiated the 30 days of Google Cloud Challenge. The community driven program aims at providing hands on cloud training via Qwiklabs with no simulation and real cloud environment.

    The quests are specifically designed with detailed instructions and demo videos to instigate smooth learning.
    An all time functioning helpdesk maintained by the DSC members is there to clear doubts and help the participants facing difficulties while performing the labs.

    On completing the quests of a particular track, A google cloud digital badge gets awarded on the qwiklabs profile page and on successful completion of the given tracks, Participants get awarded with exciting schwags and goodies from Google Cloud.

    Students need to first register and enroll themselves in the cloud program, avail credits on Qwiklabs and follow the steps required to take part in the challenge.

    It is free of cost. All it requires is dedication and exciting goodies await you.
